# Psychological Mechanisms Sustaining Abuse

This file explains the internal and relational dynamics that trap victims and enable perpetrators.

---

## Victim-Side Mechanisms

- Trauma bonding
- Learned helplessness
- Dissociation
- Hypervigilance
- Shame internalization
- Fear conditioning
- Hope reinforcement cycles

---

## Abuser-Side Mechanisms

- Entitlement belief systems
- Fragile masculinity
- Narcissistic injury response
- Authoritarian worldview
- Moral disengagement
- Externalization of blame
- Dehumanization of victim

---

## Family-System Dynamics

- Patriarchal hierarchy
- Male alliance reinforcement
- Female compliance enforcement
- Collective denial
- Honor ideology

---

## Key Insight

Abuse persists not due to weakness, but due to **systematic psychological captivity**.
