# Glossary of Key Terms

This glossary standardizes terminology used throughout the repository.

---

## Abuse
A pattern of behavior used to control, dominate, or harm another person.

---

## Narcissistic 
Several combinations of behaviors that are utilized by the abusers or family, and
friends of the abuser

---

## Coercive Control
A strategic pattern of intimidation, isolation, and domination that strips autonomy.

---

## Trauma Bonding
Emotional attachment formed through cycles of abuse and intermittent reinforcement.

---

## Femicide
The killing of a woman because she is a woman, often by an intimate partner or family member.

---

## Honor-Based Violence
Violence justified by perceived violations of family or community norms.

---

## Gaslighting
Psychological manipulation that causes the victim to doubt reality.

---

## Strangulation
Restriction of blood flow or breathing; a major predictor of lethal violence.

---

## Patriarchy
A social system where males hold primary power and authority.

---

## Learned Helplessness
Condition where repeated abuse leads to perceived inability to escape.

---

## Escalation
Progressive increase in severity, frequency, or control of abuse.

---

## Core Insight

Precise language enables precise intervention.
