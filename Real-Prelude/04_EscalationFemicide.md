# Escalation Pathway Toward Femicide

Femicide is the terminal stage of an observable escalation trajectory.

---

## Phase 1: Control Establishment

- Monitoring
- Isolation
- Economic restriction

---

## Phase 2: Psychological Breakdown

- Gaslighting
- Fear conditioning
- Dependency

---

## Phase 3: Resistance Emergence

- Attempts independence
- Seeks help
- Questions authority

---

## Phase 4: Retaliatory Escalation

- Threats
- Surveillance
- Violence intensification
- Bad Rumors Creations (dephamation)
- "Flying Monkeys" Surveillance by friends, family or close abuser relations

---

## Phase 5: Lethality Threshold

- Strangulation
- Weapon presence
- Final threats
- Collective approval

---

## Phase 6: Femicide

- Often and most of the times are premeditated, then perpetraded.
- Justified internally
- Rarely impulsive
