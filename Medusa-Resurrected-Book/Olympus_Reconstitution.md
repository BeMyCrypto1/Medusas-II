# Reconstitution of Olympus

The old Olympus functioned as:
- A closed only elite
- A patriarchal immunity zone
- A narrative enforcement engine
- A specific only 'biased' groups

It failed and continues to fail.

---

## Principles of the New Order Counsel of Echos

1. No Authority Without Accountability  
2. No Power Without Consent  
3. No Justice Without Restoration  

---

## Structural Changes

- Divine roles are assigned by ethical and tested function, not lineage
- Survivors hold judicial authority, unbiased and pegged to the Truth
- Memory is preserved, restored and not erased
- Symbols may be reclaimed by the harmed

---

## Interregnum of the Seas

Poseidon’s domain remains unassigned.
Control over forces capable of mass harm requires:
- Collective consent
- Demonstrated restraint
- Justice alignment -not emotional used responses-

There will be no rushed heir.

## Medusa’s Seat

Medusa does not Rule by Bribery, Power, nor Fear.
She governs by Recognition.

Those who meet her gaze are not destroyed anymore.
But those presented, they are unable to lie.

Medusa is installed as the Sovereign Arbiter of Transformational Justice, 
governing by Recognition—an irreversible unveiling of truth that makes deceit impossible. 

Her authority operates through:

The Impartial Core: 
Geological fairness to all nations, serpent-perception of fact/intent/consequence, 
and mirrored verdicts

The Transmuted Gaze: 
Healing through confrontation, restoration through sanctuary

Architectural Powers: 
Dismantling corrupt systems, reviewing all prior judgments, 
convening all those marginalized voices

Her justice flows like a **River's Course** reshaping landscapes. 
Her Justice Truly Prevails.
